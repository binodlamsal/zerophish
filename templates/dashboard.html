Dashboard

You haven’t created any campaigns yet. To get started:
1. Click on ‘Users & Groups’ from the left menu, then ‘New Group’ in the top right. Give the group a name and import users via .csv or add them one by one. Save the new group.
2. Click ‘Campaigns’ from the left menu, then ‘New Campaign’ in the top right. Select a template, landing page and launch date (optionally, you can spread out when messages will be sent, by also completing the ‘send emails by’ box). Select a sending domain, add your group and launch your campaign.
If you’d prefer to make your own templates, just visit “Email Templates” and “Landing Pages”.
